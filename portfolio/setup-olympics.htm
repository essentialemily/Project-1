Setup Olympics Instruction Sheets

The Setup Olympics were a series of contests run during IIT Campus and Conference Centers' annual student training, designed as simulations of actual job tasks. Attractive and fun design of the documents used to present the events helped keep new hires informed, and experienced trainees interested and motivated.
