Emily Sutherlin

Emily Sutherlin is currently working as the External Events Coordinator at the Illinois Institute of Technology's Campus and Conference Centers. She is commiited to providing excellent customer service to conference, corporate, and private event clients interested in bringing their event to our architecturally significant campus.

Emily completed her undergraduate degree at IIT's Institute of Psychology from 2003-2007, during which time she was involved in such activities as 33rd Street Productions, New Student Orientation, and Alpha Sigma Alpha. She also worked part time for the Campus and Conference Centers producing events, and the interning on the summer conference team. It was this part-time student employment that led her into her current career path, and as such, she values student employment and seeks always to develop those she supervises.

Currently, she is attending classes in the Technical Communication and Information Design master's curriculum, during which the website you are reading was a produced. She hopes to one day turn her passion for concise and clear communication into a career.

Having grown up in rural Washington State, she feels passionately about nature and conservation. She now lives with two close friends in the Uptown neighborhood of Chicago, and owns a large grey cat named Alfred.
